    • 1. What is web site?

    a group of World Wide Web pages usually containing links to each
    other and made available online by an individual, company, or organization.

    2. What is the deiffrence between http & https?

    HTTPS is more secure than HTTP because it uses encryption to protect information as
    it is being sent between clients and servers. When an organization enables HTTPS
    any information you transmit, like passwords or credit card numbers,
    will be difficult for anyone to intercept.

    3. What is the URL?

    URL is an acronym for Uniform Resource Locator
    and is a reference (an address) to a resource on the Internet.
